Audiomesh FAQs

Check out our questions and answers below:

How is Audiomesh different from iOS's Voice Memos?
Voice memos allows you to record and save your recording on your phone. You can sync the sound to your iCloud or email it to share. Audiomesh is more like Dropbox where all the sharing happens right in Audiomesh. Also, I plan to take Audiomesh to Android as well.

Can I share recordings with people who don’t use Audiomesh?
Unfortunately, no. When you share with a non-Audiomesh user, they receive an email telling them to sign up for a free account. Once they sign-up, they will automatically receive a notification that you shared the sound with them.

How much storage space do free users get?
100 MB.

Why do we have to pay for more storage?
For small companies like Audiomesh, storing your audio files on our server costs money. If I don’t charge, I don’t stay in business. Fortunately, Audiomesh is much cheaper than some of the other services available such as Netflix or Spotify.

Do sounds that other people shared with me count against my storage?
No.

Do folders I create count against my storage?
No.

How much does Audiomesh cost?
You choose your plan: $3.99 per month, $18.99 every six months, or $29.99 per year. We recommend the yearly because it’s the best deal (approximately $2.50 per month).

How secure is Audiomesh with my payment information?
Audiomesh uses Stripe to process payments. That means we never see your credit card information. Your information is sent directly to Stripe and Stripe gives us the go-ahead to update your account.

How do I delete my account?
Send an email to audiomeshapp@gmail.com from the email account you used to sign up with Audiomesh. Let me know you’d like to cancel and I’ll take care of it.

How do I downgrade to the free plan?
Send an email to audiomeshapp@gmail.com from the email account you used to sign up with Audiomesh. Let me know you’d like to cancel and I’ll take care of it.

What is the maximum file size for audio recordings?
Right now the max file size is 10 MB or approximately 20 minutes of recording. If you record any more than this, the sound won’t sync and you won’t be able to share it.
