THE PROBLEM

Musicians need at least three different apps to record, share, and store their audio files in the cloud. Then there's worrying about file formats when sharing between iPhone and Android. Not to mention creating backups of your phone in case of loss or upgrade. It’s a pain in the butt. Musicians need a better way to share and store their song ideas.

THE SOLUTION

With Audiomesh, simply record your sweet guitar riff and select the people you want to share with. Those people will receive a notification. The audio file is automatically synced to their Audiomesh app if they accept the share request. The audio file is backed up in the cloud and formatted for listening on any phone. Audiomesh combines recording, sharing, and cloud storage so you can focus on writing music.

Seriously, if Dropbox had a baby with an audio recorder, that would be Audiomesh.
